    About the AI advisor

    313flight blends two layers of intelligence:

    1. Heuristic layer: uses days until departure and the distribution of prices in your current search to guess whether this looks like a good deal.
    2. Learning layer: stores anonymized snapshots of past searches in SQLite and fits a simple trend on how prices change as the date approaches.

    When both layers agree, confidence increases. When they disagree, we tell you and lower the confidence score.

    Disclaimer: this is an educational tool. Always double-check prices across multiple sites and book with trusted providers.
