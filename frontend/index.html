Learning behaviour

313flight keeps a rolling memory (~90 days) of recent searches in Neon Postgres and builds a seasonal trend per route and month. It only makes a strong “Book” or “Wait” call when patterns are clear; otherwise it shows “No strong signal”.

About the AI advisor

The advisor combines heuristic logic with a lightweight seasonal model.

  • Heuristic layer: uses how many days remain until departure and compares current prices (min / average / max) to decide whether fares look cheap or expensive.
  • Seasonal layer: for each route and month, it tracks how prices have behaved in recent months when people searched far in advance (≥ 30 days) vs very close to departure (≤ 7 days).
  • If both layers agree, you see a strong “Book now” or “Wait” signal with higher confidence. If data is weak or inconsistent, the advisor shows “No strong signal” instead of guessing.
  • Only a lightweight summary is stored in the database, not full user-level history, and raw data older than ~90 days is automatically pruned.
